Personas and Storyboarding

Weight belts are used when gym-goers want to add extra weight for what are typically body-weight exercises (pullups, dips, etc.).

Interview Questions

  • Describe your process of putting the belt on?
  • How would you explain using this interface to a new user?
  • Was there anything that confused you during use?
  • Do you think you could figure out how to properly use this interface by yourself?
  • What do you like about this interface? What do you not like?
  • Whatâ€™s one thing you would improve about this interface?

Responses

  • Interviewees appropriately put the belt around their waists, but were not sure how to put it on afterwards
  • They said it would be very difficult to tell someone how to out the belt on without showing them directly
  • They also did not think they could figure out how to use the belt without someone else helping them
  • Interviewees liked how slim and comfortable the belt was, but did not appreciate the lack of directions
  • To improve the design, they suggested adding visual instructions and having the different parts be different colors
